Dispute Resolution

At VIP Betting Services, we are committed to providing a fair, transparent, and secure betting environment for all our clients. In the unlikely event of a dispute, our Dispute Resolution process ensures that all concerns are addressed promptly and fairly. Please review the following guidelines on how disputes are handled on our platform.

Submitting a Dispute

If you believe that an error or issue has occurred in relation to your account, bet, or any service provided by VIP Betting Services, you can submit a formal dispute. To do so, please:

  • Contact our Customer Support team within 30 days of the incident via [insert contact method such as email or support form].
  • Provide a detailed explanation of the issue, including relevant information such as your account details, the nature of the dispute, and any supporting evidence (e.g., screenshots or transaction records).
  • Our team will acknowledge receipt of your dispute and begin an internal investigation within 48 hours.

Investigation and Response

Once a dispute is submitted, our Dispute Resolution team will thoroughly investigate the issue. This process may involve reviewing:

  • Your account activity and transaction history.
  • Betting logs, relevant market information, or any technical issues that may have contributed to the dispute.
  • Any communication you have had with our Customer Support team prior to submitting the dispute.

We aim to resolve all disputes within 14 business days. However, complex cases may require additional time. In such cases, we will keep you informed of any delays and provide regular updates on the status of your dispute.

Resolution Outcome

After completing the investigation, our Dispute Resolution team will provide a final decision regarding the outcome of the dispute. This decision may include:

  • Granting compensation or adjusting account balances, if the investigation finds that an error occurred on our part.
  • Denying the dispute if it is determined that no error occurred or the issue is outside the scope of our policies.

We will notify you of the outcome via email or your preferred method of contact. Our decision is final, and further appeals may only be considered under exceptional circumstances.

External Resolution Options

If you are not satisfied with the final decision made by VIP Betting Services, you may have the option to escalate the matter to an independent dispute resolution body. We recommend contacting the relevant regulatory authority or a third-party arbitrator recognized by the online gaming industry.

Please note that VIP Betting Services is committed to resolving disputes internally in a fair and impartial manner, and we encourage clients to engage with our Dispute Resolution team first before seeking external resolution.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the Dispute Resolution process or require further assistance, please reach out to our Customer Support team at contact@vipbettingservices.com. We are here to help ensure that your concerns are handled quickly and efficiently.
